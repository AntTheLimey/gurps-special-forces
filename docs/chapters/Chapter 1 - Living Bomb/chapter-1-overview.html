Chapter 1 Overview

1

Overview

The newly recruited team of NATO special operators arrives at Voss Dynamics’ Arizona campus, is briefed on the emerging threat of fragment-empowered individuals, and dispatched on their first mission: the live capture of a young man in Afghanistan who can detonate himself like a suicide bomber and survive. Returning to the Voss Campus, the team receives Voss Combat Suits — experimental power armour — and begins training and customisation. Voss reveals a portal to an alien planet called Terra, constructed from meteor fragments, and tasks the team with a recon mission after a previous expedition vanished. The team discovers a dead alien civilisation, a hostile insectoid species, and evidence of the lost team’s fate.
