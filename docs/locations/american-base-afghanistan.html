American Base Afghanistan

Military installation

Summary

American Base Afghanistan is a NATO-controlled military installation serving as the primary debrief and operations headquarters for coalition forces in the region. The base received The Exploding Man, the high-value prisoner captured by the team during [Session 01 - Operation Living Bomb](…/chapters/Chapter 1 - Living Bomb/Sessions/session-01-operation-living-bomb.html). The facility provides secure holding, intelligence debriefing capabilities, and command infrastructure for ongoing operations in Afghanistan.

The base serves as both a military command center and a social hub for deployed personnel, with recreational facilities that allow troops to decompress following combat operations. The team’s successful mission completion culminated in celebration at the facility’s officer’s club, marking their first victory as an assembled unit.

Points of Interest

  • Prisoner Processing & Holding — Secure facilities for captured high-value detainees
  • Intelligence Debriefing Center — Interrogation and debriefing operations
  • Officer’s Club — Social and recreational facility for commissioned officers and visiting personnel
  • Command Center — Main headquarters for regional operations
  • Medical Facilities — Treatment and assessment of prisoners and personnel
  • Barracks & Quarters — Military personnel accommodation
