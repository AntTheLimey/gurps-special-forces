Las Vegas Airport

Airport terminal

Summary

Las Vegas Airport is where the team’s mission began, serving as the initial meeting point for the newly assembled Special Forces operators. The airport terminal features the typical trappings of a major gambling destination, including fruit machines scattered throughout the terminal and a lobby bar outside the security checkpoint. This mundane setting provided an unremarkable cover for the clandestine recruitment and briefing of the team.

Sam Elliott (Driver) was stationed in the terminal holding a sign with the three team members’ names, providing the first point of contact for the newly conscripted operatives. The casual airport environment allowed for discrete communication without drawing the attention of civilian authorities or interested parties.

Points of Interest

  • Fruit Machines — Slot machines throughout the terminal, typical Vegas atmosphere
  • Lobby Bar — Located outside security checkpoint, provided discrete meeting space
  • Terminal Seating — Where team members could gather before departure
  • Meeting Point — Where Sam Elliott (Driver) held the recruitment sign
