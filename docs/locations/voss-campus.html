Voss Campus

Northern Arizona › Voss Campus
Corporate compound

Summary

Voss Campus is the sprawling headquarters compound of Voss Dynamics, a high-tech defense contractor located in Northern Arizona. The facility stands as an incongruous oasis of Silicon Valley-style architecture and manicured green lawns amid the surrounding desert scrubland. The compound represents the corporate face of Voss Dynamics’ advanced weapons research and development operations, designed to present a professional corporate image while housing highly classified military work.

The campus serves as the operational headquarters and training base for the team, providing both administrative facilities and living quarters. Each recruit is provided with their own apartment on the grounds, integrating them into the Voss ecosystem and ensuring close proximity to ongoing operations and training.

Points of Interest

  • Main Corporate Building — Administrative center and public-facing headquarters
  • R&D Facility — Advanced weapons research and development laboratories
  • Medical Building — Medical facilities for team care and post-mission debriefing
  • Security Station — Controlled access and perimeter monitoring
  • Barracks — Military-style accommodation facilities
  • Barracks Mess Hall — Dining and common area for team meals
  • Garage — Vehicle maintenance and deployment staging
  • Apartments — Individual residential units for recruits and personnel
  • Second-Floor Conference Room — Briefing room for operational briefings and mission planning
  • Obstacle Course — Outdoor military training area with ropes, netting, elevated logs, eight-foot walls, wire crawls, thirty-foot cargo nets, and rope climbs. Used for morning exercise and power suit testing.
  • R&D Level -1 — High-security subterranean laboratory containing power suit storage, assembly stations, and experimental technology.
  • R&D Level -2 — Highly restricted sublevel housing The Portal to Terra. Access controlled exclusively by Voss.
