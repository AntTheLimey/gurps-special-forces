The Regenerator

Role Fragment-empowered individual Nationality Unknown Status alive

Summary

The Regenerator is a highly sought-after fragment-empowered individual with the demonstrated ability to heal almost any injury. Unlike other known powered individuals, The Regenerator has maintained an exceptionally low profile, with location and identity remaining unknown. The extraordinary value of their abilities makes them a prime target for acquisition by Voss Dynamics and presumed competing interests globally.

The individual was mentioned during the initial mission briefing as part of the threat/asset assessment landscape created by The Meteor Event. No direct contact has been made, and their current whereabouts remain unknown to the team.

Known Abilities

  • Accelerated Healing: Capacity to heal nearly any injury
  • Self-Sufficiency: Presumed ability to heal self as well as others
  • Resilience: Sustained survival through recovery of otherwise fatal wounds

Current Status

Alive and uncontained. Location and identity unknown. Actively evading detection and pursuit. Threat level: minimal (presumed benign); Value level: critical (presumed extremely high to multiple parties).
