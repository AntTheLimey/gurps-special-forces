The Exploding Man

Role Fragment-empowered individual (captive) Nationality Unknown Status captive

Summary

The Exploding Man is a young male individual with the ability to detonate explosively with the force of a suicide bomber, yet survive the detonation—though the experience leaves him exhausted. Five documented detonation incidents have occurred across the Middle East, making him a high-priority target for containment. His powers suggest a connection to The Meteor Event, though the exact mechanism of his abilities remains unknown.

During the Afghanistan operation, he proved remarkably resilient to conventional incapacitation methods. His unnaturally tough skin deflects tranquilizer darts, and he demonstrated resistance to knockout gas through exceptional constitution. He exhibits a distinct behavioral pattern before detonation: a crackling sound and brief pause, which operative Guy recognized as the trigger signature.

Physical Characteristics

  • Durability: Unnaturally tough skin, resists tranquilizer penetration
  • Detonation Pattern: Distinct crackling sound followed by brief pause before explosion
  • Detonation Survival: Can survive his own explosions, though suffers exhaustion
  • Gas Resistance: Demonstrated resistance to knockout gas (high HT rating)

Incident History

  • Five documented detonation incidents across Middle East
  • Survived each detonation with capacity to repeat
  • Exhaustion follows detonation (recovery period unknown)

Current Status

Captured by The Team during the Afghanistan mission. Currently held at a NATO base under Voss Dynamics supervision, kept sedated pending further study and interrogation.
