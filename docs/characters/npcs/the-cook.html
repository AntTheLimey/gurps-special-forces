The Cook

Role Mess Hall Staff, Voss Campus Nationality Unknown Status alive

Summary

The Cook is an overweight, greasy-looking staff member employed in the barracks mess hall at the Voss Dynamics campus. While his actual name remains unknown, he presented an opportunity for black-market dealings when operative Ronnie Vint attempted to recruit him into a supply-stealing scheme. The Cook’s apparent willingness to entertain such proposals suggests potential use as an internal asset or contact for acquiring restricted items or information from within the facility.

His role as mess hall staff provides natural access to supply logistics, inventory systems, and informal communication networks throughout the barracks. Whether the Cook is motivated by money, grievance with Voss, or simple opportunism remains unclear.
