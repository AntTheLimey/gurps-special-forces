Adrian Voss

Role CEO, Voss Dynamics Status alive

Note: Session play notes recorded this character as “Andrew Voss,” but the canonical name is Adrian Voss per player confirmation. “Andrew” is listed as an alias for cross-reference.

Summary

CEO of Voss Dynamics, the defense contractor the PCs are assigned to. Named in press coverage as the head of the company that secured a federal contract to investigate the meteor.

In Session 01, Voss personally briefed the team in a second-floor conference room at the Voss Campus. He was measured and direct, explaining the emergence of super-powered individuals following the meteor event and showing the team surveillance footage of The Exploding Man — a young man who could detonate like a suicide bomber and survive. He introduced Major Jenkins and the Voss Combat Suit program, laid out the mission parameters for the Afghanistan capture operation, and mentioned two other known supers: The Dragon and The Regenerator.

He offered $100,000 per person as a bonus for the target’s live capture, on top of the team’s $250,000 annual salary and $50,000 signing advance.
