Yi Jiangku

Role Head of Research, Voss Dynamics R&D Nationality Unknown

Summary

Yi Jiangku leads the research and development division at Voss Dynamics, overseeing the investigation into fragment-empowered individuals and the development of fragment-derived technologies. Little is publicly known about his background or qualifications, lending an air of mystery to his position. His presence is deeply unsettling—he exhibits an uncanny ability to read people, and his gaze suggests knowledge far exceeding what he should possess about the recruits.

During the initial briefing, Yi personally distributed three classified folders to the team members—“Hungry God,” “Toasty Titan,” and “Jens”—each apparently tailored to the recipient in ways that were clearly intentional. He has also conducted research into unexpected factors affecting performance, providing Major Jenkins with a music-playing device as part of an ongoing experiment.
