Sam Elliott (Driver)

Role Driver / Voss Dynamics staff Nationality American

Summary

Sam Elliott is a professional driver employed by Voss Dynamics. He bears an uncanny resemblance to the actor Sam Elliott, sharing the name but with a different spelling. He presented himself as highly professional and unflappable, paying a bar tab with a black corporate American Express card that spoke to his standing within the organization.

Elliott drove the stretch limousine carrying the newly recruited operatives from Las Vegas to the Voss Dynamics campus in Arizona. His composed demeanor and attention to detail suggested significant experience in discrete transportation assignments.
