The Meteor Event

disaster ~2019-03-11 (approx. one week before 2019-03-18 press coverage) A remote region (specific location unknown)

Summary

Around March 11, 2019, a meteor fragment struck a remote region of Earth. Initially described as a routine impact, the event rapidly escalated into a global crisis. The object displayed properties unlike anything previously observed, and individuals who came into contact with fragments began developing superhuman abilities.

What We Know (from press coverage, 2019-03-18)

The Impact:

  • Struck a “remote region” — exact location not publicly disclosed
  • Authorities immediately restricted access to the impact site
  • Federal agencies and private research groups are investigating
  • Environmental effects reported near the impact zone: power disruptions, unexplained heat signatures

Enhanced Individuals:

  • Social media reports of rapid healing, increased strength, heightened sensory perception
  • Claims remain officially “unverified”
  • Dr. Elaine Porter (biophysics) says no scientific evidence yet confirms meteor-to-biology link
  • Government has neither confirmed nor denied the connection

Corporate Response:

  • Voss Dynamics (CEO: Adrian Voss) secured the major federal contract for the investigation
  • This is the same corporation the PCs are now assigned to
  • Stock surged on the news
  • Analyst Jordan Hale implies Voss has a pattern of involvement with exotic materials

Detection Failure:

  • NASA failed to detect the object before impact
  • Cited “small size and unusual trajectory” — the trajectory explanation is potentially significant
  • Lawmakers calling for more planetary defense funding

Public Safety:

  • Federal warning: DO NOT approach or touch meteor fragments
  • Fragments may pose chemical or radiological hazards
  • National reporting hotline established

Open Questions

  • Where exactly did it strike? “Remote region” is deliberately vague
  • Is the “unusual trajectory” code for “this wasn’t a natural object”?
  • How many fragments exist and where are they now?
  • What is the mechanism of power-granting? Contact? Proximity? Bonding?
  • Can fragments be shared, split, or destroyed?
