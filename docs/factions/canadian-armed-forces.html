Canadian Armed Forces Stub

military

Summary

The unified armed forces of Canada, comprising the Royal Canadian Navy, Canadian Army, and Royal Canadian Air Force. Source of Guy LeFleur’s military career and the institution that failed him.

Role in Campaign

Background faction. Guy’s history with the CAF drives his backstory — he rose through the ranks on talent, got scapegoated for an officer’s incompetence, and now serves out a punishment posting while Lt. Nash Miller thrives on family connections.
