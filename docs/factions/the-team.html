Summary

The player character party — a small unit of special operators from various NATO nations assigned to The Defense Contractor program. This is the “my unit” referenced in Guy LeFleur’s Sense of Duty disadvantage.

Members

  • Guy LeFleur — Canadian, JTF 2 (former), demolitions specialist, extraordinarily lucky pyromaniac
  • Ronnie Vint — British, 22 SAS (Mobility Troop), CQB specialist, petty criminal
  • Jens Hartmann — German, KSK (Kampfschwimmer), combat diver / demolitions, precision engineer
  • Sammy Castaneda — “The Savage”, special ops dual-wielding pistoleer, psionic danger sense, weirdness magnet

Role in Campaign

The core PC group. Bonds formed in the field create the Sense of Duty that drives characters to risk themselves for each other.
