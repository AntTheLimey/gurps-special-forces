NATO Stub

Summary

The North Atlantic Treaty Organization. NATO member nations are the source of the PCs — elite operators assigned from various national special operations units to the defense contractor program.

Role in Campaign

NATO serves as the overarching governmental authority behind the PC assignment. Individual NATO nations contribute operators from their tier-one units (US Army Rangers, Navy SEALs, UK SAS, German KSK, etc.).
