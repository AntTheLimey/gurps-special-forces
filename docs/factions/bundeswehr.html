Bundeswehr Stub

military

Territory: Germany

Goals

    Summary

    The unified armed forces of the Federal Republic of Germany. The Bundeswehr comprises the Heer (army), Marine (navy), Luftwaffe (air force), and various joint commands. Jens Hartmann enlisted in the Bundeswehr at 19, tested into the Pioniertruppe (engineer corps), and was later selected for the Kampfschwimmer — the combat swimmer element of KSK.

    Role in Campaign

    Background faction. The Bundeswehr is to Jens what the Canadian Armed Forces are to Guy — the parent military structure. Unlike Guy’s strained relationship with the CAF, Jens’s relationship with the Bundeswehr is professional and healthy. He was selected for the contractor program on merit, not shipped out as a problem.

    Needs

    • Bundeswehr awareness of meteor-fragment threats
    • German government position on the contractor program
    • Jens’s specific unit history within the Pioniertruppe before Kampfschwimmer selection

    Appearances

    • Backstory reference in Jens Hartmann

    Relationships

    • Member of NATO — Germany is a NATO member state
    • Contains KSK — KSK (Kommando Spezialkräfte) is the Bundeswehr's special forces command, including the Kampfschwimmer
    • Employs Jens Hartmann — Jens is a Bundeswehr soldier — enlisted at 19 into the Pioniertruppe, selected for Kampfschwimmer, now seconded to the contractor program
