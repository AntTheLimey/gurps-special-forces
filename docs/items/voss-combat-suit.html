Voss Combat Suit

Summary

The Voss Combat Suit is a highly experimental full-body tactical armor system developed by Voss Dynamics using technology derived from meteor fragments. The suit resembles a sophisticated blend of military tactical gear and sleek tactical armor, with visual elements reminiscent of advanced combat uniforms. It represents the cutting edge of Voss R&D capabilities and is currently the only operational suit of its kind.

Major Jenkins is the first operative to be fitted with a Voss Combat Suit, serving as both the primary test subject and tactical asset for the team. The suit dramatically enhances Jenkins’ combat effectiveness through passive defense and active weapon systems. The team has been promised they will receive their own suits following successful completion of their first mission, suggesting Voss Dynamics intends to outfit the entire strike team with this technology.

The suit’s power source remains one of the operation’s deepest mysteries—it is powered by an unknown element derived from the meteor fragments that form part of the broader The Meteor Event. The mechanism of this power generation is not yet understood by the team.

Properties / Stats

Defensive Ratings:

  • Torso: DR 70 (passive)
  • All other locations: DR 50 (passive)

Enhancements:

  • Enhanced Strength: Provides significant boost to carrying capacity and melee damage potential
  • Enhanced Jumping: Substantially increases vertical and horizontal jumping distance
  • Integrated Weapon System: Contains Sonic Weapon

Power System:

  • Unknown element derived from meteor fragments
  • Sufficient capacity for extended operations
  • Sonic Weapon activation completely drains power reserves (see Sonic Weapon for details)

Appearance:

  • Full-body coverage tactical design
  • Visually evokes Snake Eyes tactical aesthetic mixed with Noir-influenced design elements
  • Integrated equipment mounting points
  • Sealed environmental system
