The Potato

Artefact

A drained meteorite fragment wrapped in special shielding foil to prevent tracking. Roughly the size of a baked potato. Although it no longer grants powers (99% drained), it remains rare and valuable as a research material.

Acquisition

Xander, a scientist in the R&D department at the Voss Campus, offered it to Ronnie through Eric (the mess hall cook Ronnie had befriended). The initial asking price was $10,000; Ronnie negotiated it down to a 7% cut of whatever Ronnie could eventually sell it for.

Current Status

In Ronnie’s possession. He plans to find a buyer during the upcoming R&R trip to the nearest city.
