Research Folders

Summary

Before Operation Living Bomb commenced, Yi Jiangku presented three mysterious folders to the strike team recruits. The folders were labeled “Hungry God,” “Toasty Titan,” and “Jens”—cryptic designations that have not been explained. The distribution of the folders was deliberate and psychologically significant: Yi Jiangku made direct eye contact with each recruit while presenting their respective folder, suggesting careful attention to individual psychology or foreknowledge of personal significance.

The contents of the folders remain completely unknown to the team. Guy LeFleur managed to read all three labels during the distribution, Ronnie Vint caught glimpses of two labels (“Hungry God” and “Toasty Titan”), while Jens Hartmann only glimpsed his own name on his folder. The entire situation is deeply suspicious and suggests that Voss Dynamics—and specifically Yi Jiangku—has been conducting psychological or intelligence profiling of the operatives. The fact that the first two folder labels appear to reference other entities or phenomena adds another layer of mystery.
